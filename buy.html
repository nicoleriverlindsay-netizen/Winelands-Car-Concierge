Why Use a Car Concierge?

The Smarter Way to Buy a Car

The used car market is complex, time-consuming, and full of risk. Dealers are trained negotiators. Private sellers may not disclose everything. Prices shift daily. We level the playing field — entirely in your favour.

Save Weeks of Time

Searching AutoTrader, visiting sellers, test driving disappointments — we absorb all of that. You only see pre-vetted, shortlisted options that match your criteria.

Pay the Right Price

We use live market data and professional negotiation techniques to ensure you pay a fair price — not the asking price. Our clients consistently save more than our fee.

Buy With Confidence

Every vehicle we recommend has passed our due diligence process — eNaTIS check, VIN verification, finance clearance, and independent mechanical inspection.

Access More Stock

We source from auction platforms (WeBuyCars, BidCars, Cubbi), private sellers, fleet disposals, and our own Winelands network — giving you access far beyond public listings.

The Process

How It Works

A simple, transparent four-step process from brief to keys in hand.

1

Tell Us What You Want

A quick WhatsApp chat or contact form submission covers your budget, preferred make/model, must-have features, and timeline. No forms to fill in — we keep it conversational.

2

We Search & Shortlist

Within 48 hours we deliver a curated shortlist of verified vehicles sourced from auctions, private sellers, and dealer networks. Every option has been pre-checked for title, finance status, and basic condition.

3

We Inspect & Negotiate

Once you select a preferred vehicle, we arrange an independent mechanical inspection and negotiate the best possible price on your behalf using live market benchmarks. You approve the final figure — we do the hard talking.

4

You Drive Away

We handle all sale documentation, licensing, and registration. If needed, we can arrange delivery to your door anywhere in the Western Cape. You just sign and enjoy your new car.

What We Can Find You

Any Budget. Any Segment.

We operate across all price segments in the Winelands market. Whether you're after an entry-level commuter or a premium estate vehicle, we know where to find it and what to pay.

Entry Level

Suzuki Swift, VW Polo Vivo, Toyota Starlet — reliable everyday vehicles for first-time buyers, students, and second-car needs. R160,000 – R280,000.

Volkswagen Polo Vivo

SA's best-selling hatchback — practical, affordable, and a proven performer in the Winelands. Widely available and excellent on resale. R160,000 – R250,000.

Mid-Range — Toyota Corolla Cross

The Corolla Cross is one of the fastest-growing used SUVs in the Western Cape — stylish, reliable, and perfectly sized for Winelands professionals and families. R350,000 – R550,000.

Premium & Lifestyle

Toyota Fortuner, BMW X3/X5, Land Rover Defender, Mercedes-Benz GLE — for estate owners, expats, and lifestyle buyers. R600,000 – R1,500,000.

Bakkies — Ford Ranger Wildtrak

The Ford Ranger is the #1 used bakkie in the Western Cape — built for Winelands farm roads, estate use, and open highway. Toyota Hilux and Nissan Navara also available. R350,000 – R800,000.
