Our Story

Born in the Winelands

Winelands Car Concierge was founded by Karen and Greg out of a simple observation: the people in our community were consistently overpaying for cars, underselling their own, and wasting weekends navigating a market that wasn't built in their favour.


For 13 years, Greg has been the person friends, family, and acquaintances turn to when they need to buy or sell a car. Not because it was his job — but because he's genuinely good at it and people trust him to get it right. Winelands Car Concierge was founded to make that same trusted service available to the wider community.


We work for our clients. Not for dealers. Not for platforms. For the people sitting across the table from us.
