The Process

How We Sell Your Car

A managed end-to-end selling process — from valuation to transfer.

1

Free Valuation

Send us your car's details — make, model, year, mileage, condition, and service history. We run a live market analysis and give you a realistic selling price range within 24 hours. No obligation.

2

Preparation & Photography

We advise on any quick-win preparation that could increase your sale price (valet, minor repairs). Once ready, we photograph the vehicle to a professional standard and write a compelling listing description.

3

Listed & Managed

Your car goes live across multiple platforms simultaneously. We handle all incoming enquiries, WhatsApp messages, and viewing requests — filtering out time-wasters so only genuine buyers reach you.

4

Negotiate & Close

We negotiate with interested buyers using real market benchmarks to protect your price. Once agreed, we manage the sale agreement, payment confirmation, and registration transfer. Done.

Our Advantage

Why We Get You More

Presentation matters enormously in the used car market. A professionally photographed, well-described vehicle with a verified service history commands a meaningfully higher price than the same car listed with phone photos and a vague description.

Priced with live market data

Access to our buyer network

Our existing network of buyers actively looking in the Winelands means your car may sell before it even goes public.
